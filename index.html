Welcome to SpotiGen

Spotigen is a project by Evan O'Keefe, designed to generate a playlist based off of artists

Click the "About SpotiGen" button at the bottom of your screen to learn how to use the tool

Spotigen started as an idea for my AP Computer Science A project. I was tasked to make something that included natural language processing, which is the act of taking human language, and performing functions to make the computer do something. Most people made a simple autocomplete, but I wanted to be ambitious. I set off to make a spotify playlist generator. However, I wasn't able to complete the project, as I had to use the wrong programming language. So I made this, seperately, because I couldn't just give up on my project.

I am Evan O'Keefe. At the time of making this I am a Junior at Poway High School. I am passionate about programming and game development. I started programming in 7th grade, when I taught myself C# and Unity. I now know 6 different programming languages, and have made many projects. In my free time I enjoy playing video games like Need For Speed, Counterstrike, and Fortnite, and playing music on guitar or ukulele.
